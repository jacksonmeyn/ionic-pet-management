Editing a pet record

Here you can make changes to an existing pet record. A pet's name cannot be changed.

Make sure the following rules are met:

  • A species must be selected.
  • A pet's age must be a whole number.
  • A pet must have a gender.
  • A registration number is compulsory for cats and dogs, but optional for other species.
  • A contact number must be entered.

Want to delete a pet record?

Deleting is done on the list page instead. Simply swipe the record and choose 'delete'.

Adding a pet record

Here you can add a new pet record.

Make sure the following rules are met:

  • A pet must have a name. A new pet cannot have the same name as an existing pet
  • A species must be selected.
  • A pet's age must be selected and must be a whole number.
  • A pet must have a gender.
  • A registration number is compulsory for cats and dogs, but optional for other species.
  • A contact number must be entered.
